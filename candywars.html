HOW TO PLAY
THE GOAL
You're a candy entrepreneur! Buy candy cheap in one spot and sell it for more somewhere else. You have 30 days (default) to make as much money as possible. Your final score is coins + piggy bank − what you owe your parents.
TRAVELING
Each trip to a new location uses up one day and brings new prices. Different places have different deals — shop around! Traveling also adds daily interest to your allowance advance.
BUYING & SELLING
Enter a quantity and hit BUY, or hit MAX to buy as much as your coins and bag space allow. To sell, use the quantity field in Your Bag and hit SELL. Your average cost and profit/loss % are shown for each item you're holding.
ALLOWANCE DEBT & PIGGY BANK
You may start with an allowance advance from your parents that compounds daily. Pay it off fast — at 10%/day it grows quickly! The piggy bank keeps your coins safe from random events. Click Coins or Owe Parents in the Finances panel to deposit, withdraw, borrow, or pay.
GEAR
Buy these from the Gear Shop when you can afford them:
🎫 Hall Pass ($1,500) — Reduces inspection losses. 60% chance to scare off bullies.
📻 Walkie-Talkie ($500) — Better market info — nearly full selection every visit.
🛹 Skateboard ($3,000) — 1 free trip per day with no day lost or interest charged.
🏷️ Club Card ($2,000) — Once per day, buy any item at 25% off (up to half the supply).
LOCATION SPECIALTIES
Each location has a specialty item (shown as ★) that's always available, priced ~20% cheaper, and stocked in larger quantities.
RANDOM EVENTS
Every time you travel there's a chance something happens — a principal inspection, a price surge, a shortage, a bully, a found stash, or a neighborhood tip. Gear can help reduce losses. After day 15, bigger events can happen too: a Going-Home Sale, a Buyout, an Inspection Warning, or a Club Card Declined.
LAST DAY
On the last day you cannot travel. Sell your candy, settle your accounts, then hit END GAME. You'll get a warning 3 days before the end.
