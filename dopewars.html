HOW TO PLAY
THE GOAL
You have 30 days (default) to make as much money as possible. Buy drugs cheap, sell them high, and pay off your debt before time runs out. Your final score is cash + bank − debt.
TRAVEL
Each trip to a new borough uses up one day and generates a fresh market. Prices vary wildly by location — shop around. Traveling also applies daily interest to your debt.
BUYING & SELLING
Enter a quantity and hit BUY, or hit MAX to buy as much as your cash and coat space allow. To sell, use the quantity field in Your Stash and hit SELL. Your average cost and profit/loss % are shown for each drug you're holding.
DEBT & THE BANK
You start with a loan shark debt that compounds daily. Pay it off fast — at 10%/day it snowballs quickly. The bank keeps your cash safe from muggers and events. Click Cash or Debt in the Finances panel to deposit, withdraw, borrow, or pay.
UPGRADES
Buy these from the Black Market panel when you can afford them:
🔫 9mm Pistol ($1,500) — Halves losses in a bust. 60% chance to scare off muggers.
📱 Burner Phone ($500) — Nearly full drug selection every market visit.
🚗 Stolen Honda ($3,000) — 3 free trips per day (no day lost, no interest accrued).
📞 Supplier Contact ($2,000) — Once per day, buy any drug at 50% market price.
RANDOM EVENTS
Every time you travel there's a chance something happens — a police raid, a price surge, a drought, a mugger, a found stash, or a tip from a snitch. Some events can be mitigated with upgrades.
COAT OFFERS
Occasionally after traveling you'll be offered extra carry capacity for a price. More space means bigger buys.
